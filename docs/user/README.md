## Account Security

### Protect Your Account
- **Never share your API keys** with anyone
- **Change your password regularly** (at least every 90 days)
- **Enable 2FA** on your account if available
- **Keep your email updated** for security notifications

### API Key Safety
Your API keys give full access to your account. Keep them secret!
- Don't paste keys in code repositories
- Don't share in Slack or email
- Rotate keys regularly
- Revoke old keys immediately

## Data & Privacy

### Your Data Belongs to You
- We don't use your conversations to train our AI
- We don't share your data with third parties
- You can export or delete your data anytime
- We encrypt data in transit and at rest

For detailed privacy information, see our Privacy Policy.

## Getting the Most from Meta Chat

### Best Practices
1. **Start with a clear chatbot purpose** - What will it help with?
2. **Upload quality documents** - Better documents = better answers
3. **Test thoroughly** - Ask test questions before going live
4. **Monitor conversations** - Check what users are asking
5. **Improve over time** - Update documents based on questions

### Performance Tips
- Keep documents focused and well-organized
- Remove outdated or duplicate documents
- Use consistent formatting in documents
- Keep knowledge base under 500 documents for best performance
- Update documents monthly for accuracy
