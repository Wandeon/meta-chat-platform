## Understanding Your Usage

### Billable Items

**What costs money:**
1. **Base Plan** - Your monthly subscription
2. **Additional Messages** - If you exceed monthly limit
3. **Additional Documents** - Some plans charge extra
4. **Team Seats** - Additional team members
5. **Premium Features** - Custom domains, etc.

**What's free:**
- Chatbot creation
- Settings changes
- Document uploads
- Team management

### Message Limits

Messages = conversations with your chatbot

**Examples:**
- 1 conversation = 1 message
- 10 back-and-forth = 10 messages
- Monthly reset on billing date

**Current usage:**
1. Go to Dashboard
2. See "Messages Used" counter
3. Shows X/Y (used/limit)
4. Resets monthly

### Document Limits

Documents = uploaded files in knowledge base

**Examples:**
- 1 PDF = 1 document
- 1 CSV = 1 document
- Max 10 per Free plan

**Storage:**
- Unlimited document size on paid plans
- Max 50MB per document

## Upgrading Your Plan

### Step 1: Go to Billing

1. Dashboard → Settings (gear icon)
2. Click **Billing** or **Subscription**
3. See your current plan

### Step 2: Click "Upgrade"

1. Find **Upgrade Plan** button
2. See plan options and pricing
3. Compare features

### Step 3: Select New Plan

Click the plan you want:
- Starter ($29/month)
- Professional ($99/month)
- Enterprise (custom)

### Step 4: Review

See:
- New monthly cost
- What features you'll get
- Immediate upgrade or next billing date
- Refund policy (if downgrading)

### Step 5: Confirm Payment

1. Check you entered correct payment info
2. Click **Upgrade**
3. Charged immediately (prorated if mid-month)
4. Access new features right away

### Upgrade Effective Date

**Immediate:** Features are available right now

**Billing:** 
- If you upgrade mid-month, you pay the difference
- Next full billing cycle: Standard rate
- Example: Upgrade on day 15 of 30
  - Pay 50% for remaining days
  - Then full price on day 1 of next month

## Downgrading Your Plan

### Step 1: Go to Billing

1. Dashboard → Settings
2. Click **Billing**
3. Find your plan

### Step 2: Click "Downgrade"

Not all plans show downgrade:
- You can only downgrade to lower-tier plans
- Can't downgrade from Free

### Step 3: Confirm

1. Understand what you'll lose
2. Example: Downgrading from Professional
   - Lose ability to create 10 chatbots
   - Limited to 3 chatbots (Starter limit)
   - Oldest chatbots might be disabled
3. Click **Confirm Downgrade**

### Step 4: Effective Date

- **Some changes:** Immediate
- **Billing:** Refund or credit applied
  - If downgrading mid-month
  - Credit applied to next month
  - Or refunded to payment method (5-10 days)

### What Happens if You Exceed Limits

**If you hit message limit:**
1. Chatbot stops responding
2. You see warning: "Message limit reached"
3. Options:
   - Upgrade plan
   - Wait for next month
   - Purchase additional messages

**If you exceed documents:**
1. Can't upload new documents
2. See warning: "Document limit reached"
3. Options:
   - Delete old documents
   - Upgrade plan
   - Consolidate documents

## Payment Methods

### Add Payment Method

**Step 1: Go to Billing**
1. Settings → Billing

**Step 2: Click "Payment Methods"**
1. See list of saved cards
2. Click **Add Payment Method**

**Step 3: Enter Card Info**
- Card number
- Expiration date
- CVV (3-digit code on back)
- Billing address

**Step 4: Save**
1. Click **Add Card**
2. Card is saved securely
3. Used for future charges

### Remove Payment Method

If you don't want to use a card anymore:

1. Go to Payment Methods
2. Find the card
3. Click **Remove** or delete icon
4. Confirm removal

**Note:** If it's your only card, add another first.

### Update Expiring Card

When a card is about to expire:

1. Remove old card
2. Add new card
3. Set as default
4. Future charges use new card

## Billing Troubleshooting

### Card Declined

**Usually means:**
- Incorrect card number
- Card expired
- Insufficient funds
- Bank blocked transaction

**Fix:**
1. Check card number is correct
2. Check expiration date
3. Verify funds available
4. Contact your bank (they might be blocking)
5. Try different card

### Charge Appeared But No Access

**If you were charged but features don't work:**

1. Refresh page (Ctrl+R)
2. Log out and log back in
3. Wait 5 minutes
4. Check "Current Plan" in Billing

**If still not showing:**
1. Go to Billing
2. Check payment status (paid/pending)
3. If pending, wait 2-3 hours
4. If failed, retry payment
5. Email support@metachats.ai with invoice number

### Cancel Subscription

To stop being charged:

**Step 1: Go to Billing**
1. Settings → Billing
2. Find **Cancel Subscription**

**Step 2: Click Cancel**
1. You'll see: "Are you sure?"
2. Reason (optional): Tell us why
3. Click **Cancel Subscription**

**Step 3: Confirm**
1. Last payment: No new charges
2. Access: Until billing cycle end
3. After: Reverts to Free plan

**Example:**
- You pay $29 on Jan 1
- You cancel Jan 15
- You keep Starter features until Feb 1
- No charge on Feb 1
- Reverts to Free

### Refunds

**If you were overcharged:**

1. Email support@metachats.ai
2. Include invoice number
3. Explain issue
4. We review and issue refund
5. Refund takes 5-10 business days

**If you upgraded and want refund:**

- Free trial period: Full refund if within 7 days
- After 7 days: No refund on used period
- Downgrade instead: Get credit for unused time

## Using Free Plan

### Free Plan Limits

- 1 chatbot
- 10 documents
- 100 messages/month
- 1 team member
- Basic support

### Upgrade When?

Consider upgrading when:
- You need more than 1 chatbot
- You hit message limit (100/month is small)
- You have more than 10 documents
- You need team members
- You need API access
- You need advanced features

### Extending Free Plan

To make free plan last longer:
1. Only upload necessary documents
2. Merge documents together
3. Delete old/unused documents
4. Consolidate chatbots
5. Publicize less to limit messages

**Note:** This isn't sustainable long-term. Most users upgrade within 1-2 months.
